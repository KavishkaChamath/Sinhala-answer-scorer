# Questions and Marking Guides - Ancient Sri Lanka (Anuradhapura Period)
# ප්‍රශ්න සහ ලකුණු ලබාදීමේ මාර්ගෝපදේශ - පුරාණ ශ්‍රී ලංකාව (අනුරාධපුර යුගය)

---

## Question 1 / ප්‍රශ්නය 1
**Sinhala:** අනුරාධපුර යුගයේ බෞද්ධ ආගමේ භූමිකාව සවිස්තරව විස්තර කරන්න.
**English:** Describe in detail the role of Buddhism in the Anuradhapura period.

### Marking Guide (20 marks):
1. Buddhism's arrival - Mahinda Thero's mission, King Devanampiya Tissa's conversion (247 BCE) - **4 marks**
2. Religious monuments - Thuparamaya, Ruwanwelisaya, Jetavanaramaya, Abhayagiri - **4 marks**
3. Role of monasteries and Sangha in education, culture, and social welfare - **4 marks**
4. Writing of Pali Canon (Aluvihara) and Mahavamsa chronicle - **4 marks**
5. Buddhism's influence on kingship, state policy, and national identity - **4 marks**

---

## Question 2 / ප්‍රශ්නය 2
**Sinhala:** අනුරාධපුර රාජධානියේ වාරිමාර්ග ශිෂ්ටාචාරය ගැන ඔබ දන්නා දේ විස්තර කරන්න.
**English:** Describe what you know about the irrigation civilization of the Anuradhapura kingdom.

### Marking Guide (20 marks):
1. Importance and purpose of irrigation for rice cultivation and population support - **4 marks**
2. Key tanks and builders (Tissa Wewa/Devanampiya Tissa, Minneriya/Mahasena, Abhaya Wewa/Pandukabhaya) - **4 marks**
3. Bisokotuwa (sluice) technology - unique engineering achievement - **4 marks**
4. Interconnected tank systems, canals, and water distribution networks - **4 marks**
5. Irrigation as religious duty, royal responsibility, and merit-making - **4 marks**

---

## Question 3 / ප්‍රශ්නය 3
**Sinhala:** රජ දුටුගැමුණු ශ්‍රී ලංකා ඉතිහාසයට කළ දායකත්වය විස්තර කරන්න.
**English:** Describe the contributions of King Dutugamunu to Sri Lankan history.

### Marking Guide (20 marks):
1. Military campaign - unification of Sri Lanka, defeating King Elara - **4 marks**
2. Religious monuments built - Ruwanwelisaya (Mahathupa), Mirisawetiya, Lohaprasada - **4 marks**
3. Significance of defeating Elara for Sinhala Buddhist national identity - **4 marks**
4. Role in establishing Buddhist state and patronage of religion - **4 marks**
5. Legacy recorded in Mahavamsa and his place in Sri Lankan historical memory - **4 marks**

---

## Question 4 / ප්‍රශ්නය 4
**Sinhala:** අනුරාධපුර රාජධානියේ පාලන ක්‍රමය පිළිබඳව විස්තර කරන්න.
**English:** Describe the administrative system of the Anuradhapura kingdom.

### Marking Guide (20 marks):
1. King as supreme ruler - role, duties, and divine status - **4 marks**
2. Council of ministers (Amatyas) and their advisory roles - **4 marks**
3. City organization - planned capital with distinct zones, founded by Pandukabhaya - **4 marks**
4. Provincial administration - regional governors and local management - **4 marks**
5. Justice system and social order maintained by the king - **4 marks**

---

## Question 5 / ප්‍රශ්නය 5
**Sinhala:** අනුරාධපුර රාජධානිය පරිහානියට පත් වීමට හේතු සහ ඒ රාජධානියේ උරුමය ගැන ලියන්න.
**English:** Write about the causes of the decline of the Anuradhapura kingdom and its legacy.

### Marking Guide (20 marks):
1. South Indian (Chola) invasions - repeated attacks and military pressure - **4 marks**
2. Internal power struggles, succession conflicts among princes - **4 marks**
3. Vijayabahu I's resistance and eventual transfer of capital to Polonnaruwa (1017 CE) - **4 marks**
4. Architectural, religious, and cultural legacy - monuments still standing today - **4 marks**
5. Hydraulic civilization legacy - irrigation systems that influenced later kingdoms - **4 marks**
